Shopping

Unique shops, boutiques & local markets

World Fashion Centre Amsterdam

A hub for the fashion industry, offering showrooms, shops, and events related to clothing and textiles. World Fashion Centre Amsterdam is a fashion enthusiast's dream. Immerse yourself in the latest trends and explore a wide range of clothing and textile products.

Fruittuin van West

A fruit orchard and farm where visitors can pick their own fruits and enjoy the rural surroundings. Fruittuin van West offers a delightful escape to the countryside. Spend a day picking fresh fruits and savoring the tranquility of the orchard.

Gathershop

A concept store offering a curated selection of lifestyle products, including home decor, accessories, and gifts. Gathershop is a treasure trove of stylish finds. Explore their thoughtfully curated collection and find the perfect memento of your Amsterdam adventure.

Hutspot

A concept store offering a unique mix of fashion, design, and lifestyle products from emerging brands and designers. Hutspot is a hub of creativity and style. Explore their carefully curated selection of fashion and design items, and discover the next big thing in the world of Dutch design.

Ten Kate Market

A bustling street market offering a wide variety of products, including fresh produce, flowers, clothing, and specialty foods. Ten Kate Market is a feast for the senses. Stroll through the lively market, sample local delicacies, and soak up the authentic Amsterdam atmosphere.

Mashed Concept Store

A lifestyle store featuring a blend of fashion, accessories, and home goods, with a focus on sustainable and ethical products. Mashed Concept Store is a treasure trove of conscious living. Discover stylish and eco-friendly items that reflect the best of Dutch design and craftsmanship.

Hoodlamb

A sustainable clothing brand known for its stylish and eco-conscious outerwear, made from hemp and recycled materials. Hoodlamb is a pioneer in sustainable fashion. Explore their collection of high-quality, earth-friendly outerwear, and make a fashion statement with a clear conscience.

Friday Next

A lifestyle store offering a curated selection of furniture, home accessories, and fashion items, with a focus on Dutch design. Friday Next is a treasure trove of design inspiration. Explore their thoughtfully curated collection and find the perfect pieces to add a touch of Dutch sophistication to your home.

Bootiez Concept Store

A trendy concept store offering a unique mix of fashion, accessories, and lifestyle products from emerging designers and brands. Bootiez Concept Store is a must-visit for fashion-forward shoppers. Discover the latest trends and unique pieces that reflect the best of Dutch design and creativity.

The Otherist

A lifestyle store offering a unique mix of fashion, accessories, and home goods, with a focus on vintage and one-of-a-kind items. The Otherist is a treasure trove of eclectic finds. Explore their collection of vintage and unique pieces, and add a touch of personality to your wardrobe and home.

The American Book Center

A renowned bookstore offering a wide selection of English-language books, including fiction, non-fiction, and children's books. The American Book Center is a haven for bookworms. Browse through their extensive collection of books, and find the perfect read to accompany your Amsterdam adventure.

The Darling Market

A pop-up market featuring a rotating selection of fashion, accessories, and lifestyle products from emerging designers and brands. The Darling Market is a must-visit for fashion enthusiasts. Discover the latest trends and unique pieces, and support up-and-coming designers and brands.
