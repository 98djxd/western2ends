Restaurants

Discover diverse lunch & dining options

Midtown Grill

Steaks, burgers, and seafood lovers, you're going to love Midtown Grill. Tucked inside the classy Amsterdam Marriott Hotel, this place is all about serving quality grub. It's got a modern, elegant vibe, and the staff are just the right mix of friendly and attentive. You can't help but feel a bit fancier when you're dining here!

Restaurant Adam

Now here's a place that takes your dining experience up a notch. At Restaurant Adam, they whip up a 5-course menu that switches up every month, all with fresh and seasonal ingredients. It's intimate and cozy, making it the perfect spot for a romantic dinner. If you're looking for a unique culinary journey, this is your stop.

Truffle

Who doesn't love a good French dish? Truffle gives you that but with a modern spin. They're all about using fresh, high-quality ingredients to create dishes that are bursting with flavor. It's a warm, welcoming place - you'd feel right at home. Plus, the staff really know what they're doing, and they're always ready to make your dining experience even better.

Meneer Nieges

Picture this: a classic brasserie near the scenic Westerpark, serving delectable seafood dishes. That's Meneer Nieges for you. It's a cozy, relaxed spot, and its location along the water makes it perfect for those summer evenings. There's just something about enjoying a meal here that makes everything feel right.

Viscafé de Gouden Hoek

If you're a seafood fan, then you're going to fall in love with Viscafé de Gouden Hoek. Known for their fresh and mouth-watering fish dishes, this restaurant has a charming, cozy atmosphere that will make you feel right at home. The staff is friendly and attentive, making sure your dining experience is top-notch.

Mossel & Gin

This isn't just your typical food restaurant. Mossel & Gin specializes in mussels and other fish dishes, but with a twist – they offer an impressive selection of gin and tonics to pair with your meal. The ambiance is cozy and relaxed, and the knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist.

Waterkant

For a taste of Suriname in Amsterdam, look no further than Waterkant. Serving up authentic Surinamese dishes that are absolutely delicious, this restaurant is conveniently located in a renovated multi-storey car park. The atmosphere is casual and relaxed, making it the perfect spot for a fun night out with friends.

Himalayan Kitchen

Step into Himalayan Kitchen for a culinary adventure. Renowned for their Nepalese and Indian dishes, you'll experience flavors that are spicy and rich, thanks to their use of fresh, high-quality ingredients. The restaurant exudes a cozy and welcoming vibe, with a friendly and attentive staff to ensure a satisfying dining experience.

La Casa Di Michael

For lovers of Italian cuisine, La Casa Di Michael is a must-visit. They serve up delicious, authentic Italian dishes using fresh, high-quality ingredients. The atmosphere is cozy and warm, making it the perfect place for a romantic dinner.

Cafe Zurich

Cafe Zurich is a trendy cafe that offers delicious food and drinks. They have a large terrace overlooking the water, perfect for a summer evening. The atmosphere is lively and bustling, perfect for a casual night out with friends.

Orontes West

Orontes West is a Turkish and Mediterranean restaurant that offers delicious and authentic dishes. They use fresh and high-quality ingredients to create flavorful and traditional dishes. The restaurant has a cozy and warm atmosphere, perfect for a night out with friends.

Cafe Restaurant Amsterdam

Cafe Restaurant Amsterdam is a trendy restaurant located in a former water pumping station. They offer a wide variety of dishes, from seafood to steak to vegetarian options. The atmosphere is stylish and sophisticated, perfect for a special occasion.
